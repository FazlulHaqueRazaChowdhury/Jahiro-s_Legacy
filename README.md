## 🕹️ Gameplay Overview

> Survive endless waves of **Shony's minions** and protect Jahiro’s legacy.

- 🔁 **Infinite Survival Mode** — No limits. Just skill.
- ❤️ **Health Reset Mechanic** — Every 5 kills restores full health *but increases difficulty*.
- 🎯 **Dynamic Combat System** — Vector-based aiming, shooting, and ammo management.
- ✨ **Visual Feedback** — Particle effects and real-time HUD enhance immersion.
